Objective & Strategy

Objective: Launch SONEX brand in Malaysia through a dedicated cookware container. Different logistics profile from FMCG — heavy (8,500 kg), high-value single brand, requires 40ft FCL.

Why Separate: Weight-to-volume ratio makes mixing with FMCG impractical. Cookware needs dedicated stacking and protection. Single brand simplifies customs classification to one HS code.

Services Used: Sourcing (manufacturer liaison), Export Docs (proforma, packing list), Freight (40ft FCL booking), Quality (pre-shipment pressure rating inspection).
